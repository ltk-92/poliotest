Samsung Engineering Receives the WangNoi Gas Plant Contract from PTT

Samsung Engineering was awarded an EPCC contract from PTT Public Company Ltd., a Thailand state-owned oil & gas company, for a gas compressor station project worth $110mil USD. The project is located in WangNoi, Ayutthaya 70km north of Bangkok, Thailand.The project which uses Gas Turbine Driven Compressors with Shaft Power of 16MW will provide additional capacities of 800 MMSCFD gas for the city of Bangkok. TAKREER PRE Package#3 U&O Project
  • UAE
  • REFINERY

In October 2009, Samsung Engineering won a contract for the Fertil-2 ammonia/urea project to build what was at the time the largest plant of its type in the UAE. The project was awarded by Fertil, a joint venture between Abu Dhabi National Oil Company (ADNOC) of the UAE and TOTAL of France. As of late 2000, ADNOC planned a number of projects aiming to turn the country’sabundant natural resources into high value-added businesses. As part of the plan, Fertil was established with a 2:1 shareholding ratio (ADNOC/TOTAL). The Fertil-2 project, awarded by Fertil, is able to treat 2,000 MTPD of ammonia and 3,500 MTPD of urea a day.
